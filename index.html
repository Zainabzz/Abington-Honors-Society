About

A Community of Engaged Scholars

Penn State Abington offers two honors pathways for academically exceptional students — the Abington Honors Program and the university-wide Schreyer Honors College. Both provide opportunities to take honors courses, pursue original research, and be part of an active scholarly community.

Academic Distinction

Honors graduates receive special recognition at commencement and a notation on their official Penn State transcript.

Research Opportunities

Work one-on-one with a faculty mentor on an ACURA undergraduate research project and present findings at the campus symposium.

Enriched Curriculum

Take specially designed honors sections of existing courses, or propose an honors option with any willing instructor.

Schreyer Honors College

Penn State's most prestigious honors program — open to new students with exceptional academic records. Schreyer Scholars complete a senior thesis and enjoy exclusive scholarship opportunities.

Community & Leadership

Participate in honors retreats, community service, and networking with faculty and alumni who share your passion for learning.

Eligibility

Students with a cumulative GPA of 3.4 or higher are encouraged to apply. Contact the Honors coordinator to discuss your goals.
